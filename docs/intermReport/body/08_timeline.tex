\textbf{\large Summary:}\\[0.4cm]
Before December 4, we completed the majority of our research tasks and model deployment. From December 4 until the end of the winter break, we will focus on website or application development. Our goal is to finish approximately 90\% of the project before the start of the second semester, and devote the second semester to testing and refinement, including but not limited to improvements based on questionnaire feedback.
